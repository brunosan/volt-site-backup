Change the name of your website

You can change the organization name displayed on your NationBuilder site at any time.
Written by Kev O'Connor
Updated over a week ago

Go to Website > Site settings > Basic. Save your changes at the bottom of the page by clicking Save site.

Remember to change the name of your organization under Settings > Contact. Learn more about changing the name of your nation.
