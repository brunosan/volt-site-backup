Help Volt to change the way politics is done for a united Europe.

The rise of nationalism and populism shows that Europe is at a crossroads. We want to stand up for a prosperous, united European future (Read our programme here). What makes us unique is our focus on citizen empowerment. We are locally present in all European countries to actively contribute to our democracy by connecting citizens across Europe. In the last European Elections in May, over half a million European voters believed in our vision of a united, federal Europe. We now have our first Member of the European Parliament in Brussels.

Volt chooses a United Europe

Now it is time to invest in our volunteers and to give them the tools to organise local communities, to run ground-breaking bottom-up campaigns and create the political change driven by people that Europe needs. We want our volunteers to learn from the best! Marshall Ganz, the lead strategist of Obama's grassroots campaign, trains talented citizens to transform them into community organisers and campaign leaders at the Harvard Kennedy School. We have carefully selected 19 talented volunteers who will have the opportunity to enroll in this course! With your support, we aim to sponsor 50% of their tuition fees. (Read about our candidates below) With your support, our volunteers will go to Harvard!

Help us build a united Europe where citizens are the shapers of our future! No matter how small your donation is, help us to take our volunteers to Harvard! Thank you in advance for your donation and participation in our movement of EMPOWERING Europe.
