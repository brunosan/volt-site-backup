Your control panel is where you do the vital work of finding, organizing, and equipping your community. Our job at NationBuilder is to make the experience of doing that work as smooth, easy, and beautiful as possible—so you can focus on leading.

Incorporating usability research and customer feedback, we’ve meticulously redesigned each NationBuilder interface to create a more streamlined and powerful user experience. We’re thrilled to share a brand new control panel, grounded in a unified design system that we will continue to improve and build upon over time.

Designed with love to help you lead

An interface that expands to your full browser width, providing more room to work

Drastically improved consistency across interfaces and layouts to help you move faster

Distinct navigation hierarchy to orient and ground your experience
